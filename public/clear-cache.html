1 Quick Hard Refresh

Mac: Press Cmd + Shift + R

2 Clear Browser Cache (Chrome/Edge)

1. Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
2. Select "Cached images and files"
3. Choose "All time" from the time range
4. Click "Clear data"

3 Clear Browser Cache (Firefox)

1. Press Ctrl + Shift + Delete
2. Select "Cache"
3. Choose "Everything" from the time range
4. Click "Clear Now"

4 Open Developer Tools Console

1. Right-click on the page ‚Üí "Inspect"
2. Go to "Network" tab
3. Check "Disable cache" checkbox
